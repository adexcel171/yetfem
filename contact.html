FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability.
How do I confirm my booking?
Submit your booking online to get a reference number, then make a payment to our Moniepoint account and send proof of payment via WhatsApp with your reference number. We confirm within 30 minutes.
Is breakfast included in the room rate?
Breakfast is available at our restaurant and can be added to your stay for an additional fee. Please let us know when booking.
Is parking available at the hotel?
Yes! We offer complimentary, secure on-site parking monitored by our 24/7 security team for all registered hotel guests.
Do you allow pets?
We currently have a no-pets policy to ensure the comfort of all our guests. Contact us if you have specific needs.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations at least 48 hours before check-in are eligible for a full refund. Within 48 hours may incur a cancellation fee.
