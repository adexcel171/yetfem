FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability — please contact us in advance.
How do I confirm my booking?
To confirm your booking, make a payment to our Moniepoint account and send proof of payment via WhatsApp along with your check-in date, room type, and number of nights. We'll confirm within 30 minutes.
Is breakfast included in the room rate?
Breakfast is available at our restaurant and can be added to your stay for an additional fee. Please let us know when booking if you'd like to include it.
Is parking available at the hotel?
Yes! We offer complimentary, secure on-site parking monitored by our 24/7 security team. Parking is available for all registered hotel guests.
Do you allow pets?
We currently have a no-pets policy to ensure the comfort of all our guests. Please contact us directly if you have specific needs and we will do our best to accommodate you.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made at least 48 hours before check-in are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation.
