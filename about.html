Our Story

Yetfem Hotel was born from a simple but powerful vision — to create a world-class hospitality experience right here in the heart of Abuja, Nigeria. What started as a dream to bring boutique luxury to Bwari has grown into one of the city's most beloved hotel destinations.

Our founder built Yetfem on the belief that great hospitality is not just about luxurious rooms and fine dining — it's about making every single guest feel seen, valued, and at home. That ethos permeates every corner of our property.

Today, Yetfem Hotel stands as a testament to what Nigerian hospitality can be — a seamless fusion of contemporary elegance, warm personal service, and deep pride in our culture and heritage.
