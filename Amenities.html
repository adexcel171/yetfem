Our Services

World-Class Facilities

Every detail has been carefully considered to ensure your stay is nothing short of exceptional

High-Speed WiFi

Stay connected with our complimentary ultra-fast internet available throughout the entire property — in rooms, lobbies, restaurants, and outdoor areas.

Restaurant & Bar

Savour an exquisite blend of local Nigerian cuisine and international dishes prepared by our experienced chefs, paired with a curated selection of beverages.

Business Center

A fully equipped business center and conference facilities for corporate guests and executives — including printing, meeting rooms, and high-speed internet access.

Secure Free Parking

Complimentary, 24-hour monitored parking with ample space for all guests. Your vehicle is safe and protected throughout your stay.

Luxury Bathrooms

Each room features a beautifully appointed en-suite bathroom with premium toiletries, fluffy towels, and walk-in rain showers. Executive Suites include a jacuzzi.

24/7 Security

Your safety and peace of mind are our top priority. Our property is monitored around the clock with trained security personnel and CCTV surveillance systems.

24/7 Room Service

Enjoy the luxury of in-room dining at any hour. Our dedicated room service team is available round the clock to fulfill all your culinary desires.

Laundry & Ironing

Professional laundry, dry-cleaning, and ironing services available daily. We ensure your wardrobe is always fresh and impeccably presented.

Daily Housekeeping

Our attentive housekeeping team ensures your room is pristinely maintained every day. Fresh linens, towels, and immaculate spaces are always guaranteed.
